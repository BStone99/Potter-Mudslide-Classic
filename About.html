About the Potter Mudslide Classic

The Potter Mudslide Classic isn’t just a tournament — it’s a celebration of summer, friendship, and full-throttle fun. Tucked into the heart of the property that also hosts a dirt bike track and epic backyard adventures, this event is proudly dreamed up, owned, and operated by the people who live right on the land it’s held on. This is more than just a volleyball location. It's the home base for unforgettable memories that only happen when the right people come together in the right place!

Together with one of their closest lifelong friends, the property owners put their hearts into building an experience that’s fun, inclusive, and completely down and dirty! Every detail about this event is planned and carried out with the players and best possible experience in mind.

So whether you're diving in to win it all, yelling form the sidelines, or just here fo rth emud and music, you're part of something special, and we welcome you to the fun! The Potter Mudslide Classic is a vibe, a community, and a celebration we're pumped to share with you. Let's make some memories!
